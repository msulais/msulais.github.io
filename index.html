About me

My name is Muhammad Sulais. Usually called Sules, Sulis, or Sulais (I personally prefer to be called Sules). Born in Kediri, Indonesia in 2003. My current hobbies are programming, design, anime, and games.

In terms of design, I usually design logos, icons, backgrounds, mobile & web UI (user interface). Usually, I made a design because it was for the needs of my programming project, not for sale to other people.
